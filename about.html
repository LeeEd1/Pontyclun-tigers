Meet Emma

Greetings, I'm Emma, a Tae Kwon Do enthusiast. From the tender age of 4, I embarked on a journey in this martial art and achieved the rank of 1st dan. Additionally, I proudly held the title of world champion. In 2018, I initiated the Tigers lessons, which have attracted a substantial number of followers. My ultimate goal is to inspire as many people as possible to join us, as the invaluable life lessons imparted through Tae Kwon Do are unparalleled. I invite you to attend one of our lessons, guaranteeing you a rewarding experience!
