About Us

Meet Emma

Hello there, I'm Emma, an avid enthusiast of Tae Kwon Do. Ever since I was a young child of 4, I embarked on a remarkable journey in this martial art, working hard to attain the prestigious rank of 1st dan. Along my path, I also had the honor of being recognized as a world champion, a title I hold with immense pride. In 2018, I took the initiative to establish the Tigers program, which has since gained a significant following. These lessons have captured the attention of numerous individuals seeking the transformative power of Tae Kwon Do. My ultimate aspiration is to ignite inspiration within as many people as possible, inviting them to join our community. The invaluable life lessons and personal growth fostered through Tae Kwon Do are truly unparalleled. I extend a warm invitation to you to attend one of our sessions, assuring you a deeply rewarding experience. Come and witness firsthand the incredible benefits of Tae Kwon Do, both physically and mentally. Together, let us embark on a journey of self-discovery, resilience, and empowerment.
