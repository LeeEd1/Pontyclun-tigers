Welcome to Pontyclun Tigers

About Us

Pontyclun Tigers, a division of Steve Tottle Taekwondo, is a prominent TaeKwonDo club in Wales. Established in 2018, Pontyclun Tigers was created as an additional class for Steve Tottle Taekwondo, with a focus on children aged 4-6 years old. Located at our specialized Martial Arts Centre, Pontyclun Tigers is dedicated to the growth and development of 3-6 year olds in the art of Martial Arts, incorporating play and enjoyable activities. With the recent inauguration of our martial arts center, we are now able to offer a wider range of classes. The first of these classes is a dedicated Family Beginners Class, which serves as a stepping stone for young Tigers to progress into the main Syllabus. Additionally, this class welcomes beginners of all ages, providing them with a small, personalized environment tailored to their needs. Tae Kwon Do is derived from an ancient form of unarmed combat that has been practiced for centuries in the Orient and perfected in Korea. Over the course of 2000 years, Tae Kwon Do has evolved into a mental and physical discipline, with the ultimate goal of promoting mental and physical well-being. This encompasses the health and well-being of ourselves, our families, and our friends.

Benefits Of Tae Kwon Do

Physical Fitness

Taekwondo training involves a wide range of movements, including kicks, punches, blocks, and stances. Regular practice improves cardiovascular fitness, strength, flexibility, balance, coordination, and overall physical conditioning

Self-Defense

Taekwondo is an effective self-defense system that teaches practical techniques for protecting oneself in real-life situations. Students learn how to generate power, execute strikes, and apply self-defense strategies to neutralize threats and defend against attackers.

Discipline

Taekwondo prioritizes discipline, self-control, and respect for oneself and others. Through structured training, students acquire the ability to follow instructions, cultivate self-discipline, and abide by a code of conduct diligently.

Character Development

Taekwondo training emphasizes the development of positive character traits such as perseverance, integrity, humility, and indomitable spirit. These qualities can translate into other areas of life, fostering personal growth, resilience, and a strong moral foundation.
