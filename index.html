Welcome to Pontyclun Tigers

About Us

Pontyclun Tigers is part of Steve Tottle Taekwondo, one of the largest TaeKwonDo clubs in Wales. Pontyclun Tigers started in 2018 focusing on age 4-6 year olds as a additional class of Steve Tottle TaeKwonDo. Based at our dedicated Martial Arts Centre, Pontyclun Tigers continues to grow and develop 3-6year olds in the art of Martial Arts through play and lots of fun. With the opening of our martial arts centre we are able to offer more classes, the first of which is a focussed Family Beginners Class. This is the stepping stone for Little Tigers into the main Syllabus as well as enabling beginners of all ages to join a small class focussed solely on them. Tae Kwon Do is a version of an ancient form of unarmed combat practiced for many centuries in the Orient, perfected in its present form in Korea. Tae Kwon Do is a mental and physical discipline designed over 2000 years with the ultimate goal being mental and physical health. That is the health of ourselves, our family and our friends.

Benefits Of Tae Kwon Do

Physical Fitness

Taekwondo training involves a wide range of movements, including kicks, punches, blocks, and stances. Regular practice improves cardiovascular fitness, strength, flexibility, balance, coordination, and overall physical conditioning

Self-Defense

Taekwondo is an effective self-defense system that teaches practical techniques for protecting oneself in real-life situations. Students learn how to generate power, execute strikes, and apply self-defense strategies to neutralize threats and defend against attackers.

Discipline

Taekwondo emphasizes discipline, self-control, and respect for oneself and others. Through structured training, students learn to follow instructions, develop self-discipline, and adhere to a code of conduct.

Character Development

Taekwondo training emphasizes the development of positive character traits such as perseverance, integrity, humility, and indomitable spirit. These qualities can translate into other areas of life, fostering personal growth, resilience, and a strong moral foundation.
